The system has a plant, a dial, and exactly one route up

Two hormone inputs (melanocortin, estrogen) converge on a single intracellular controller. That controller is the only path that raises pigment body-wide. The synthesis machinery below it is the plant — break it and you go down, never up.

Melanotan II works precisely because it injects the one signal this architecture is built to accept — not because it acts on skin genes at all.

More than one hand on the switch — still only one switch

Estrogen reaches melanocytes through the membrane receptor GPER1 (melanocyte cis-eGene, q≈0.005) — not ERα (excluded, melanocytes are ERα-negative by IHC) — and feeds into the identical cAMP→CREB→MITF node the melanocortin arm uses. This is the mechanistic backbone of melasma / pregnancy hyperpigmentation.

Illustration of mechanism only, not medical advice. Melanotan II sold online is unapproved and non-selective, with documented adverse effects (nausea, mole darkening that can mask melanoma monitoring). The FDA-approved, MC1R-selective cousin is afamelanotide ("Melanotan I"), used for erythropoietic protoporphyria — a different, regulated compound.
honest limits
What this is not claiming
  • The mechanism→direction rule ("losing a positive melanin regulator lowers pigment") is close to the definition of albinism — a methodological check, not a discovery on its own.
  • "No gene switches pigment up everywhere" means no melanocyte-autonomous gene — 8 systemic routes exist (6 endocrine/ACTH-excess, 2 metabolic/Wilson-ATP7B), acting through the same hormonal logic this pitch describes.
  • MFSD12 loss reduces pheomelanin and is associated with darker skin — never "loss → lighter."
  • Primate coat-colour dichromatism (15× independent origins, polygenic) is a separate evidence track, not merged into this architecture claim.
The pigment system has a floor and one key. Melanotan is that key, in a syringe.

What did Claude Science help us do

Re-annotated ~200 disorder genes into a direction×spread×cell-of-origin grid, ran three adversarial verification passes, corrected raw gene counts to real entity counts (vitiligo/VKH GWAS swarms inflated counts ~3×), PMID-checked every NB13 hormone edge, and ranked 753 network genes on population constraint — each step reviewed and layered, not a single unattended pattern-match.

How are we closer to a finding

The disorder grid converts "why can't you just make skin darker with a cream" from folklore into a structural fact: the empty quadrant plus the LOEUF split show the up-route is architecturally singular and hormonal, not genetic-and-local — and the evolutionary rank of MC1R vs MITF explains why that one route is druggable at all.

What is the flagship

The control diagram itself: one plant (melanin synthesis, disposable, break-only-down), one actuator node (cAMP→CREB→MITF, evolutionarily locked), two convergent hormone inputs, and one real-world drug that is nothing but that diagram, injected.
